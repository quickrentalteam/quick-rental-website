Browse and Filter Task



Step 1: Click on the Search tab on the footer or header or the 'Search' button.


Step 2: You are taken to the filter page where you enter your preferred choices for an apartment.


Step 3: You click 'Browse'.


Step 4: 'Browse' takes you to the results page that has your preferences.


Step 5: You click on the apartment you wish to see more details of.


Step 6: You are taken to the details page of that apartment, in which you can scroll through the photos on the slider, view full details, photos, map, virtual tour and reviews. As well as call/message the landlord or message them directly through a chat app.


Step 7: When you click on the 'Tour' button, it takes you to the virtual tour of the apartment in which you can virtually explore the entire apartment.


Step 8: The virtual tour is very interactive where you can walk through the entire apartment.


Step 9: When you click on the 'Map' button, it takes you to the map of the apartment in which you can see the walking and driving distance from the apartment to U.W.I.


Step 10: When you are back on the details page, you can call/message the landlord, or talk to the landlord directly through a chat app.

Apartment Listing



Step 1: Upon logging in, you are taken to the home page which has a list of featured apartments that you can browse through.


Step 2: You may also see a listing of apartments after entering specified criteria on the Filter page

Reviews



Step 1: On the details page, you click on 'Reviews'.


Step 2: You are taken to the reviews page where you can see the overall reviews as well as individual reviews. You can also leave a like/comment and view comments on the reviews.


Step 2.1: You are taken to the reviews page where you can see the overall reviews as well as individual reviews. You can also leave a like/comment and view comments on the reviews.


Step 3: When you click on 'Post Reviews' you are taken to the post review page where you can enter you review and upload any photos you wish to.
