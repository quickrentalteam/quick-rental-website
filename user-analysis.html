User Analysis

At the University of the West Indies, finding the right apartment is extremely difficult and time-consuming since many apartments are not advertised, outside of walking distance and not secure. Additionally, landlords are difficult to contact and featured landlords are simply unpleasant . Students must take great care and effort to locate an apartment that fits within their budget and level of comfort.

Users | Stakeholders

Our target audience for the apartment rental application would be anyone that rents an apartment at or nearby UWI; students, parents, staff, employees and landlords(for listings of their apartments).

Our user personas are as follows

01

Amanda

  • UWI Undergraduate Student.
  • Twenty-three years old.
  • Lives in South.
  • Has a hard time finding good apartments, mainly looks for apartments via driving around UWI.
  • Very time consuming and stressful, does not often find apartments that are wanted.
  • The affordable apartments advertised that are close to UWI are in horrible condition and not secure.
  • User would love if all apartments near UWI were in one catalogue to browse through.

02

Richard

  • UWI Undergraduate Student.
  • Nineteen years old.
  • Lives in Freeport.
  • Has 8am classes. There is two hours of traffic to reach to UWI from 6 - 8am.
  • Needs an apartment to be able to attend all classes.
  • Finds apartments on social media. Social media is often misleading, and the apartment does not look as advertised.
  • Stressed out to find a good apartment and wishes apartments near UWI were advertised better.

Brian

  • UWI Masters Student and part-time employee.
  • Twenty-six years old.
  • Lives in South.
  • Has to work from mornings and go to classes in the evenings.
